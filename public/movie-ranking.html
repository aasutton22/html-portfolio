The Best Movies According to Austin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Benchwarmers

A comedy about 3 nerdy adults that play in a baseball tournament against notoriously mean youth baseball teams.

Dodgeball: A True Underdog Story

A comedy about a group of average people playing in a dodgeball tournament in an attempt to win money to save their gym.

Big Hero 6

A cute animated movie about a kid using technology he invented to avenge his deceased brother.
